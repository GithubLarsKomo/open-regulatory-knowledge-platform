# VISION.md

## Vision

Create a Regulatory Knowledge Operating System that stores regulatory information as structured, versioned, traceable data objects and automatically generates technical documentation, regulatory dossiers and post-market reports.

## Guiding Principles

1. Single Source of Regulatory Truth.
2. Structured data before documents.
3. Human accountability before AI automation.
4. Traceability by design.
5. Auditability by default.
6. API-first integration.
7. Modular implementation.
8. Regulatory output remains compatible with Word, PDF, XML, eSTAR and authority-specific submission formats.
